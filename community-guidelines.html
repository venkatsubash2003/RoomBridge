Community guidelines

Be honest, respectful, and inclusive. Do not harass, discriminate, impersonate, post deceptive listings, pressure anyone for advance payment, expose private information, or move suspicious transactions outside the service.

Report unsafe behavior or misleading content. RoomBridge may restrict content or accounts after review, and affected members can use the appeal process.

For immediate danger, contact local emergency services; RoomBridge is not an emergency-response service.
